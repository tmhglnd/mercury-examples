// === TUTORIAL 230: White & Pink Noise ===
// The noise instrument allows you to create different
// types of noise, as a starting point for your sound design
// Below we have 2 basic noise generators, white and pink noise

set tempo 105

new noise white time(1/16) shape(1 100) play(euclid(16 11)) scope()

new noise pink time(1/4) shape(1/4 1) scope()
